E-commerce platform with role-based access for Customers, Sellers, and Admins.

## 🚀 Features

### For Customers
- ✅ Browse products
- ✅ Search & filter
- ✅ Shopping cart
- ✅ Checkout process
- ✅ Order history

### For Sellers
- ✅ Manage products (CRUD)
- ✅ Process orders
- ✅ Sales analytics

### For Admins
- ✅ User management
- ✅ Product moderation
- ✅ Platform analytics
